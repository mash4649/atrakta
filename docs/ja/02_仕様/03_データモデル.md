## `.atrakta/events.jsonl`

- append-only
- 各イベントに `schema_version` と `hash` を保持
- `prev_hash` でチェーン接続
- Plan3 拡張イベント:
  - `context_resolved`
  - `repo_map`
  - `routing_decision`
  - `policy_applied`
- parity/integration 拡張イベント:
  - `projection_rendered`
  - `projection_drift_detected`
  - `integration_checked`
  - `integration_blocked`
- imported capability lifecycle 拡張イベント:
  - `capability_imported`
  - `capability_analyzed`
  - `capability_quarantined`
  - `capability_promoted`
  - `recipe_candidate_created`
  - `recipe_conversion_reviewed`
  - `memory_surface_assigned`
  - `memory_promotion_reviewed`
